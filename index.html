02A — Products
Product 01 / 02
FREELANCE
CFO
Financial command center for freelancers
Live
Open app ↗

Freelancers don't get predictable paychecks. One month it's $8,000. The next it's $900. Every existing finance tool assumes you have a salary. FreelanceCFO was built for the feast-or-famine reality — answering the one question every freelancer dreads:

"IF I STOPPED GETTING CLIENTS TODAY — HOW LONG COULD I SURVIVE?"

Runway calculator with burn-rate projection & visual timeline
Irregular income budgeting — no fixed paycheck assumed
Federal + self-employment tax estimation, quarterly schedule
Client profitability tracking by effective hourly rate
